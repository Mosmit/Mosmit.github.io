About Me

I am a passionate and driven biochemist with strong expertise in molecular biology, biophysics, and cancer research. Holding a First-Class degree in Biochemistry and a growing academic and research background, I am committed to uncovering innovative solutions in oncology through collaborative, data-driven, and personalized approaches. I aim to leverage modern laboratory tools and molecular techniques to drive translational research and meaningful scientific impact.

Beyond the laboratory, I am currently a recipient of the Power Learn Project scholarship, where I am acquiring the skills necessary to become a proficient software engineering. My vision is to integrate my scientific expertise with software engineering to address real-world challenges in Nigeria, across Africa, and globally.

I am actively involved in mentoring, public speaking, digital literacy advocacy, volunteering, and exploring the intersection of software engineering and blockchain technology with biochemistry. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to advance healthcare, research, and innovation.
